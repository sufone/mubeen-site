What is Mubeen?

In the name of Allah, the All-Merciful, the Mercy-Giving,

Peace be upon you, and the mercy of Allah and His blessings

Mubeen is an app to help readers understand the main content of the Quran's surahs. This help's one to understand and remember its meanings more easily, insha Allah.

One often finds either deep and detailed tafsirs of the Quran, or short summaries of each surah to give one an overview of the fundamentals. Our hope with Mubeen is to be in the middle; so that you are able to grab all the key content and narratives within the surah, in a simplified manner, as well as the overarching themes and messages.

Therefore, what this is NOT, is any new kind of tafsir or new original piece of work – as we are not qualified for this. The following are a list of sources which we are citing and have tried to summarise from:

  • The themes section for every surah was taken mainly from Dr. Abdul Haleem's translation of the Quran
  • The context section was taken from Maududi's Tafhim al Quran
  • The headings and content were largely taken from the website islamreligion.com

Alhamdulillah, the app's contents have been approved by a qualified shaykh. We hope that this will benefit anyone who reads it insha Allah.

From the technical perspective, we have done our best to make this app: fast, simple, beautiful, and powerful. It has no ads or tracking.
